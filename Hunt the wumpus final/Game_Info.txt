THE WUMPUS LIVES IN A CAVE OF 20 ROOMS. EACH ROOM HAS THREE TUNNELS LEADING TO OTHER ROOMS. (THINK OF A DODECAHEDRON)
EACH ROOM IS CONNECTED TO THREE OTHER ROOMS WHICH YOU CAN ACCESS THROUGH THE CONNECTED TUBES.

-HAZARDS
BOTTOMLESS PITS: TWO ROOMS IN THE CAVE HAVE A PIT IN THEM, WHEN YOU ENTER IN SUCH A ROOM YOU WILL FALL, DIE AND LOSE.
WUMPUS: THE WUMPUS IS NOT BOTHERED BY HAZARDS, HOWEVER IF YOU ENTER THE SAME ROOM AS WUMPUS YOU WILL BE EATEN.
BATS: THE BATS IN THIS CAVE WILL PICK YOU UP IF YOU ENTER THE SAME ROOM AS THEM, ONCE THAT HAPPENS THE BATS WILL DROP YOU IN A RANDOM ROOM.

-ABILITIES
MOVE: YOU CAN MOVE TO A CONNECTED ROOM THROUGH A TUNNEL.
SHOOT: YOU HAVE 5 ARRORWS WHICH YOU CAN SHOOT TO SPECIFIC ROOMS, BUT BE AWARE SHOOTING AN ARROW WILL SCARE THE WUMPUS WHICH MAKES IT MOVE.
SENSE: WHENEVER YOU ARE TWO ROOMS AWAY FROM THE WUMPUS YOU CAN SENSE IT'S PRECENSE, ALSO BATS AND PITS CAN BE SENSED ONE ROOM AWAY.

-EXTRA'S
HINT ROOM: A RANDOM ROOM WITH A HINT ABOUT ONE OF THE HAZZARDS IN THE GAME, YOU CANNOT SENSE THE HINT ROOM.
AI: ITS ALSO POSSIBLE FOR AN AI TO SOLVE THE GAME FOR YOU, WHICH TAKES BETWEEN 2 AND 4 ROUNDS TO WIN.

-GAME MODES
AT THE BEGINNING YOU CAN CHOOSE BETWEEN NORMAL MODE OR CUSTOM MODE.
RANDOM MODE IS THE DEFAULT GAME MODE IN WHICH YOU GENERATE A NEW CAVE.
CONFIGURATION MODE MAKES IT POSSIBLE TO REPLAY THE GENERATED MAP CURRENTLY SAVED IN THE MAP.TXT FILE.

IF YOU HAVE TO INTERRUPT THE COMPUTER'S RUN, THEN YOU CAN DO SO BY PRESSING CTRL + C, OR CLOSING THE TERMINAL.
IF YOU WANT TO CHECK THE DATA THATS CURRENTLY ON THE SCREEN CLICK ON THE SCREEN TO PAUSE AND PRESS ENTER TO CONTINUE.

ONCE YOU HAVE REACHED THE END STATE OF THE GAME, YOU'LL HAVE THE ABILITY TO SEE THE ROUND INFO AND ALSO THE GENERAL PLAYER STATISTICS
